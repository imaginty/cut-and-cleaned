About Us

Welcome to Cut and Cleaned

Where passion for the outdoors meets professional expertise. Founded in early 2024 by three lifelong friends, our journey began in the heart of Chaldon, Surrey, where we grew up exploring and appreciating the beauty of natural open spaces. This shared love for the outdoors, coupled with our collective experience inspired us to create a company that would help others enjoy and maintain their own outdoor spaces.

Meet the Team

Hi, I'm Conrad

I grew up in Chaldon, where I developed a deep appreciation for creative, hands-on work that naturally led me to a career in carpentry. Since earning my certification in 2016, I've refined my craft as a qualified carpenter, accumulating over eight years of experience. Services

At Cut and Cleaned, we provide a comprehensive range of external maintenance services, including pressure washing, hedge cutting, garden care, external carpentry, and much more. Our mission is simple: to deliver top-quality work with a friendly approach, ensuring our customers are not just satisfied, but delighted with the results.

Exterior Cleaning
- Pressure washing
- Chemical infused Pressure Washing to enhance cleaning power and remove deeper stubborn stains and blackspot removal etc
- Rendered and brick wall cleaning
- Softwashing including Fascia and Soffits, Conservatory and Gutter clearances etc.
- Moss and Algae removal
Gardening
- Lawn care, planting, maintenance services, regular maintenance contracting, mulching and soil conditioning etc.
- Tree pruning
- Turfing and Astroturfing
Hedge Cutting
- Professional hedge trimming and shrub shaping services
Carpentry
- Decking, Fencing, Gates, Custom woodwork, Timber sleeper planters and walls etc.
Paving repointing services
- Professional removal of old jointing material and replacement with durable mortar or resin compound to enhance the appearance and longevity of your paved surfaces
Waste removal
- Fully licensed to dispose safely and responsibly of all types of waste, including garden debris, old furniture, and general rubbish
Garden clearance
- Garden clearance services designed to help you reclaim your outdoor space. Whether you're tackling a small project or a larger overhaul, our team is equipped to handle all your debris and clutter needs
